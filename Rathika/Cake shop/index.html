About Us

Welcome to Cake Delight, where every cake is crafted with love and a touch of magic! Our journey began in 2021 with a simple mission: to bring joy to every celebration through our delightful cakes and sweet treats.
Founded b Rathika, Cake Delight started as a small home-based bakery fueled by a passion for baking and a dream to create something truly special. With a family recipe book and a lot of heart, [Founder's Name] set out to create cakes that not only taste amazing but also bring smiles to faces of all ages.
At Cake Delight, our mission is to spread happiness, one delicious cake at a time. We believe that every occasion, big or small, deserves to be celebrated with a cake that's as delightful to look at as it is to eat. From birthdays and weddings to anniversaries and just-because moments, we’re here to make your celebrations sweeter.
