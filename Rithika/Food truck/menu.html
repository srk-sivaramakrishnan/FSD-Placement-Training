MENU


Desserts


Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried dumplings made of milk solids,
soaked in sugar syrup.

Rasgulla

Soft and spongy dumplings made of paneer
(Indian cottage cheese), soaked in sugar syrup.

Kulfi

Creamy Indian ice cream made with condensed milk
and flavored with cardamom, pistachios, or saffron.


Sides


Naan/Roti

Traditional Indian bread baked in a clay oven.

Raita

Yogurt mixed with grated cucumber,
tomatoes, and spices.

Papad

Crispy thin lentil wafers, served as a side orbr appetizer.

Mixed Pickle

Assorted pickled vegetables and spices.

Appetizers/Snacks


Samosas

Crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes

Pakoras

Assorted vegetable fritters dipped in

with tamarind chutney.

Bhel Puri

A tangy and spicy mixture of puffed rice,
chopped onions, tomatoes, and
chutneys.

Pani Puri/Golgappa

Hollow crispy shells filled with spicy

and onions.

Aloo Tikki Chaat

Crispy potato patties topped with

Main courses

Masala Chai
